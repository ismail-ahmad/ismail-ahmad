Who We Are

Havenwood Academy is a long-term residential treatment center that specializes in treating young women and their families who are struggling with complex trauma and attachment related issues. Our holistic approach to treatment includes specialized therapies, experiential opportunities, accredited education, life-skills programming and more. We work to pair our cutting-edge trauma treatment with creative funding sources to help young women who might not otherwise received help.

Here's what you need to know

  • We work with young women from age 12 to 18
  • We specialize in treating early complex trauma and attachment related issues
  • We often work with adoptive families
  • We offer a multi-faceted approach to treatment utilizing every therapeutic modality and technique that is beneficial to our young women and their families
  • We offer accredited education with experiential opportunities
  • We are licensed by the Utah Department of Human Services, Division of Licensing
  • We are accredited by the Joint Commission on Accreditation of Health Care Organizations
  • We accept Medicaid Insurance, California Adoption Assistance funding, regular medical insurance policies and private pay funding

Our Program

Research Based Therapy

Havenwood Academy uses evidence-based therapies to deal with complex trauma. We regularly use Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR), Dialectic Behavioral Therapy (DBT) and Brainspotting; all of which are well researched and proven trauma-specific therapeutic interventions. We often pull information and methodology from leading and pioneering researchers including Dr. Bruce Perry, Dr. Dan Siegel and Dr. Bessel van Der Kolk to ensure that we stay on the cutting edge of the best practices in our specialty.

We also utilize Equine therapy based on the Equine Assisted Growth and Learning Association (EAGALA), the gold standard for equine therapy. EAGALA therapy incorporates horses that are highly attuned to and offer a safe space for clients to make communication and connection breakthroughs.

All of our therapeutic methods, systems and interactions are trauma-informed and carried out with connection, attunement and care. Every treatment plan is specifically and carefully designed to best respond to and treat each young woman’s struggles and address her needs. Every treatment plan is carefully supervised by our Treatment Team consisting of our Clinical Director, Therapists, Program Director and Educational Director. This Team meets weekly to review each young woman’s progress in the program and the effectiveness of her therapy.

We utilize a multi-therapeutic approach under the supervision of the Treatment Team that includes individual, family, group and equine therapy on a weekly basis. We believe that “treatment” is an integrated approach combining therapy with academics and life skills designed to promote future success and personal empowerment. The combination of all of Havenwood Academy’s therapeutic methods has been shown to create positive, long-term results.

Alex Baird - Therapist

Alex Baird received her undergraduate degree in psychology from Utah State University and her master’s degree in marriage and family therapy from Salt Lake City’s Argosy University, in 2011. Alex’s passion for helping young people and their families, led her to working in residential treatment centers for adolescents and she has been doing so since 2013. Alex’s areas of expertise include depression, anxiety, attachment disorders, oppositional and defiant behaviors, emerging personality disorders, addictions, and trauma. Alex is well versed in Dialectical Behavioral Therapy and relational therapy.
